# Financial Policy & Revenue Targets — FY2024
Board Approved: December 2023

## Annual Revenue Target
**$570,000** total net revenue for FY2024.

## Quarterly Breakdown
| Quarter | Target    | Notes                              |
|---------|-----------|------------------------------------|
| Q1      | $125,000  | New year ramp period               |
| Q2      | $138,000  | Spring enterprise cycle            |
| Q3      | $142,500  | Mid-year push                      |
| Q4      | $164,500  | Holiday + year-end deals           |

Growth target: **12% QoQ** (quarter over quarter).

## Corrective Action Threshold
Any quarter achieving less than **80% of its quarterly target** triggers a mandatory
Corrective Action Report (CAR) due within 15 business days of quarter close.
The CAR must include: root cause analysis, remediation plan, and Q+1 forecast revision.

## Product Mix Targets
- Software products: **≥ 60%** of total revenue
- Services: **≤ 25%** of total revenue
- Hardware: **≤ 15%** of total revenue

## Segment Targets
Enterprise segment must account for **≥ 55%** of total bookings.
SMB segment: 30%–40%. Consumer: ≤ 10%.

## Revenue Recognition Policy
Revenue is recognized upon delivery and customer acceptance per ASC 606.
SaaS subscriptions: ratably over the subscription term.
Professional services: upon completion of defined milestones.
