# Employee Handbook — FY2024
## Section 4: Leave Policies

### Parental Leave
All full-time employees with 12+ months of tenure are eligible for **16 weeks** of paid
parental leave. Part-time employees receive pro-rated leave based on their FTE ratio.
Leave must be requested a minimum of 30 days in advance via the HR portal.
Both primary and secondary caregivers are eligible.

### Annual Leave
- < 5 years tenure: **20 days** per year
- 5+ years tenure: **25 days** per year
- Carry-over limit: 5 days. Unused leave beyond this is forfeited on December 31.
- Leave requests must be approved by your direct manager at least 2 weeks in advance.

### Sick Leave
- 10 days per year, non-accruing
- Medical certificate required for absences exceeding 3 consecutive business days

### Bereavement Leave
- Immediate family: 5 paid days
- Extended family: 3 paid days
