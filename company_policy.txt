Last Updated: January 2024
========================================

SECTION 1: REFUND & RETURN POLICY
-----------------------------------
Customers are eligible for a full refund within 30 days of the original purchase date.
Partial refunds (50%) are available for requests made between 31 and 60 days post-purchase.
No refunds are issued after 60 days unless the product is confirmed defective by our QA team.

For SaaS subscriptions, pro-rated refunds apply if cancellation is within the first billing
cycle. Annual plan subscribers who cancel after 30 days receive store credit.

Process: Submit via customer portal with your order ID. Processing: 5–7 business days.

SECTION 2: FINANCIAL APPROVAL THRESHOLDS
------------------------------------------
- Under $5,000:      Department Manager
- $5,001–$50,000:    Vice President
- $50,001–$250,000:  CFO
- Over $250,000:     Board-level approval

SECTION 3: EXPENSE REPORTING
------------------------------
All business expenses must be submitted within 30 days of incurrence.
- Meals: Up to $75 per person
- Hotels: $250/night (Tier 1 cities), $175/night elsewhere
- Flights: Economy under 4 hrs; Business class for 4+ hrs domestic, 8+ hrs international
Receipts required for all expenses over $25.
